Marvel Cinematic Universe

In the early 2000s many people were not used to or even familar with superheroes. What we knew back then about them was that they were these characters with special abilities and they all wore stretchy pants. In those days, superheroes were not all what they are hyped up to be. As we look at what came next we find that superheroes were given a complete makeover. In 2008, the first adaptation of a Marvel Comic was made. Iron Man was brought to the big screen. Because of the ratings and reviews that followed its release, the world went crazy about superheroes. Marvel made it known at the end of Iron Man, during the post-credits, that there would be more to come in what is now call the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
